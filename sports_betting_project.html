Sports Betting Project

Summary

The goal of this project was to analyze different betting strategies in regards to sports betting. Some of these strategies are based off of "gambler's fallacy" casino strategies such as doubling your bet size after an unsuccessful wager. Another strategy that was tested was a Statistical approach that predicted winners of matches using a Poisson Distribution.

What is a bet and what are the guidelines for modeling?

A bet consists of a wager on a single match between 2 teams. The bet is not complex, "I bet $10 that Team A will beat Team B". If Team A loses the match then I lose the $10. But if team A wins the match then I get a payout based on Team A's odds of winning.
