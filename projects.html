Sports Betting Project

The goal of this project was to analyze conventional betting strategies and their effectiveness when applied to sports betting. Over the past decade, many States have removed their restrictions on sports betting. The industry has started to boom with the rise of Fantasy Football and other new ways of betting on sports. This project analyzes some strategies that casual sports gamblers might consider as well as a Poisson Distribution based algorithm for a more statistical approach. Please click below to learn more.
