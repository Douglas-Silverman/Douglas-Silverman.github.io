Sports Betting Project

The goal of this project was to analyze conventional betting strategies and their effectiveness when applied to sports betting. Over the past decade, many states have removed their restrictions on sports betting. The industry has started to boom with the rise of Fantasy Football and other new way of betting on sports. This project analyzes some strategies that casual sports gamblers might look and their effectiveness. Additionally, a Poisson Distribution based algorithm was also used as a more statistical approach to gambling. Please click below to learn more.
